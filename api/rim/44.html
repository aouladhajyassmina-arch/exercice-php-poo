Help Center !

🔍1. Searching for a Book

To find a book:
Use the search bar at the top of the page
Search by title, author, genre, or keyword
Use filters such as Availability, Category, or Publication Year
Click any book to view details: summary, number of copies, and availability

📗 2. Checking Availability

On each book page you will see:
Available — the book is ready to be borrowed
On Loan — someone has borrowed it
Copies Available — how many copies are left
Return Date — when an unavailable book is expected to be returned

📘3. Reserving a Book

If a book is not currently available, you can reserve it:
1. Open the book’s page
2. Click “Reserve”
3. Choose your pickup location (if required)
4. Confirm your reservation
5. You will receive a notification when the book is ready

📚4. Borrowing a Book

You can borrow a book in two ways:
A. Borrowing a Physical Book
Visit the library
Present your library card or ID
Pick up the reserved book or take an available one
Borrowing period is usually 14 days
B. Borrowing a Digital Book (if available)
Click “Borrow Online”
The book will open in your online reader
The digital loan expires automatically after the due date

5. Loan Duration & Limits

Standard loan period: 14 days
You may borrow up to X books at a time
You can renew a loan unless the book is reserved by someone else
Late returns may result in temporary borrowing restrictions

🔁 6. Returning a Book

For Physical Books
Return your book at the library desk
Or use the Book Return Box if available
Your account will update automatically after the return

🔄7. Renewing a Loan

To renew a book:
1. Go to My Account → My Loans
2. Click “Renew Loan”
Renewal is possible if:
The book is not reserved by another user
You have not reached the renewal limit

👤8. My Account

Your account lets you:
View your current loans
Check due dates
Manage reservations
Renew books
Review your loan history
Update your personal information

❓9. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

* Do I need an account to borrow books?
Yes. You must create an account or have a library card.
* What if the book I want is already borrowed?
You can reserve it, and we’ll notify you when it becomes available.
* Why can’t I renew my book?
Because someone else has reserved it or you reached the renewal limit.
* What happens if I return a book late?
Your account might be temporarily restricted from borrowing.
* Can I cancel a reservation?
Yes, go to My Account → Reservations → Cancel Reservation.

🛠️10. Troubleshooting

Book won’t open (digital)
Refresh the page
Try a different browser
Check your internet connection
Can’t log in
Make sure your email is correct
Reset your password
Not receiving notifications
Check your spam folder
